## ✅ Per-Iteration Self-Check

Run this after every iteration. 1 min max. Be honest.

```
Iteration #___

[ ] I added exactly ONE feature (not zero, not three)
[ ] I time-boxed it (≤15 min)
[ ] I wrote the exact prompt I gave to AI
[ ] I recorded what happened (success/fail/partial)
[ ] I took a screenshot or screen recording
[ ] I committed with NAIM format: [NAIM: Name] Feature - Xkg
[ ] I updated MOBILE.md before moving to next iteration

Quick score (circle): 😤 Struggled | 😐 OK | 😎 Smooth | 🔥 Nailed it
```

## 🎁 Hidden Bonuses

Not in the rubric — but the instructor notices:

- Documenting a **failed** iteration honestly (+5)
- Identifying a **specific AI weakness** with evidence (+5)
- Helping a classmate (if mentioned in their MOBILE.md) (+3)
- Trying a **tool you've never used** before (+3)
- Reflection that teaches the instructor something new (+5)
- **Self-assessment accuracy:** If self-score is within ±10 of instructor score (+5)
